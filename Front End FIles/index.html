About Us

Game Tracker intends on making management of your video games easier. previously, you could have games installed on many storage mediums and your console would forget about them as soon as you unplug your drive. That can become a major issue with games getting larger and drive sizes stagnating. This is what we intend on fixing with game tracker.

Game Tracker is a web based application that helps you manage your game library with ease. Wether you are a big game collector making sure you aren't buying the same copy of a game or a gamer with many games spaning many storage mediums.
